Concrete Quality Assessor Pty Ltd was built on a single conviction: that every South African property developer, contractor and homeowner deserves to know — with complete certainty — that the concrete in their structure is safe, compliant and built to last.

WHAT WE DO

Complete Concrete Quality Solutions

From the first slump test to your final compliance certificate — we cover every quality checkpoint your project needs.

SANS 5863

Concrete Cube Testing

28-day compressive strength testing to verify your concrete meets specified grade requirements. Certified results accepted by all major banks and municipalities.

SANS 5862

Slump & Flow Testing

On-site workability assessment ensuring your concrete is correctly batched and placeable without compromising final structural strength.

Schmidt Hammer / UPV

Non-Destructive Testing

Assess in-situ concrete strength without core extraction — ideal for existing structures, dispute resolution and pre-purchase assessments.

SANS 10100

Mix Design Verification

Independent verification that your mix design achieves target strength, workability and durability requirements before large pours begin.

Full Supervision

On-Site Quality Control

Dedicated QC technician embedded on your site for full pours — real-time monitoring, sampling and immediate reporting to your site foreman.

Bank & Council Ready

SANS Compliance Reports

Formal compliance certificates and test reports accepted by NHBRC, all major South African banks, municipalities and structural engineers.

Forensic Analysis

Failure Investigation

Expert analysis when concrete fails — identify root cause, quantify extent, and provide a remedial action plan backed by documented evidence.

Independent Oversight

Third-Party Inspections

Neutral, independent concrete quality oversight for developers, banks, insurance assessors and legal proceedings across the Western Cape.
